Washlytics | Making Car Washes Sparkle (Digitally!)

Challenge: Car washes juggling appointments and customer info manually.
Solution: Washlytics! A full-stack system built using Next.js & TypeScript (frontend magic by TailwindCSS & Framer Motion!) and a robust backend (Supabase & REST APIs) to automate bookings, track customers, and streamline the whole operation.
Result: Less paperwork, happier managers!

Technologies Used

  • Next.js
  • TypeScript
  • Supabase
  • Framer Motion
  • TailwindCSS
